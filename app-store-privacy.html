Privacy Policy

Effective date: 12 March 2026

These apps do not collect personal information.
ArchBIM add-ins distributed via the Autodesk App Store do not transmit, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your local machine.

Scope

This policy applies to all ArchBIM add-ins available on the Autodesk App Store, including Survey Coordinates, Sheet Transfer, and Wall Sleeves.

What the add-ins do not do

  • They do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information.
  • They do not transmit any data to ArchBIM or any third party.
  • They do not include analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.
  • They do not access files outside the active Revit project.

Trial period

Add-ins obtained from the Autodesk App Store include a free trial period. Trial state (start date and a verification token) is stored locally in the Windows registry at HKCU\SOFTWARE\ArchBIM. This information never leaves your machine and is not accessible to us.

Contact

For any questions about these add-ins or this privacy policy, please contact us at support@archbim.co.in.
